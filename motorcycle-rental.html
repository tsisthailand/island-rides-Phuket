Rental terms

  • Daily, weekly (7 days), 2-week (14 days), and monthly (30 days) rates available — longer rentals get better value.
  • Newer bikes, with after-sales support if anything comes up during your rental.
  • Delivery / pick-up available, fee depends on area and bike size — free or reduced if you collect at the shop.
  • Payment: cash, card, or bank transfer / Wise. Upfront or monthly for long-term rentals.
Exact daily/weekly/monthly rates depend on the bike model, season, and current stock — see current rates on each bike above, or message us on WhatsApp for today’s rate.

Where you can ride

  • 160cc and smaller: Phuket island, up to 60km outside radius.
  • 350cc / 750cc only: Phang Nga and Krabi allowed, 146km maximum distance, extra fee applies.
  • Leaving Phuket without prior permission, or riding beyond Krabi, results in a fine — ask us first if you’re planning a longer trip.
  • No sub-renting, no riding under the influence of alcohol or drugs.

Policies

  • Rental fees are non-refundable. Deposit is refunded within 24 hours after return if there’s no damage or rule violation.
  • Late return: 150 THB/hour. More than 4 hours late is charged as one extra day.
  • Renter is responsible for damage/repair costs and towing, even if it exceeds the deposit.
  • Extensions must be requested at least 24 hours in advance to avoid a late penalty.
